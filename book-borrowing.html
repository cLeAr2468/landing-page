Book Borrowing

Easily borrow books and resources from the PIT Learning Resource Center.

Borrowing Process

For Onsite

  • Every student must deposit his/her School ID upon borrowing book(s) for home use or for photocopying outside the library. Only three (3) books of different titles (2 references and 1 Fillipiniana) are allowed to be borrowed for overnight/home use.
  • Borrowing of books starts in the afternoon, but if the need arises and the book is essentially needed, it can be borrowed anytime they want.

Note: No borrowing of books is allowed 1 week before the schedule of exam (midterm/finals). This is to lessen the chances of books not being returned in-time.

  • Photocopying outside the library should be done in not more than 15 minutes. Exceeding the given time is allowed on a case-to-case basis.

The following types of library materials are to be read only in the library:

  • General References: encyclopedias, dictionaries, atlases, and yearbooks.
  • Periodicals: magazines, journals and newspapers.
  • Vertical File Materials: newspaper clippings, newsletters, and brochures
  • Theses and Dissertations copies

Online Borrowing and Checking –In

Online Borrowing

  • Students and faculty members may loan out library materials by following the circulation guidelines;
  • Select the title of books available using the PIT-LRC Destiny Library Manager:
    http://lms.pit.edu.ph
  • Note: If assistance is needed, leave a message on the PIT-Learning Resource Center FB page or through Messenger.
  • Visit to PIT-LRC Facebook Page and type the title, author, and copyright date of the book(s) you want to borrow.
  • Wait for a notification from the librarian or personnel in charge
  • Messages will be entertained from 8:00 AM-5:00 PM Mondays to Fridays.
  • Borrower will be notified via text message, or call when the materials are ready for pick-up.
  • Present PIT ID or Official Enrollment Sheet upon picking up the materials.
  • Pick-up time is from 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM Monday – Friday.
  • A representative is allowed to pick up the book(s) upon presenting an authorization letter with ID from the borrower.
  • Fill out the book card and leave it to the Library in charge.
  • Patrons are allowed to borrow two (2) books for a period of one (1) week.
  • All borrowed materials can be renewed for another one (1) week provided that there will be no prior request from another patron. This can be done online through e-mail, text, or FB messenger.

Checking In of Library Materials

  • Patron shall drop the book(s) at the BOOK DROP BOX located at the Public Assistance Center, near the Guard House.
  • Acknowledgement messages will be sent to the patron via e-mail, text, or FB messenger.

Request for Reservation of Library Materials

  • A patron who wants to borrow library materials that are currently borrowed by another patron may request for reservation through the PIT-LRC Facebook Page or messenger.
  • Requested materials will be ready within two (2) working days after the approval of the request.
  • Failure to pick up the materials after two (2) days given period will mean forfeiture of the request. Books will be returned to its shelves.

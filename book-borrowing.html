Book Borrowing

Easily borrow books and resources from the PIT Learning Resource Center.

How to Borrow a Book

Step 1: Present ID

Show your valid school ID at the library entrance and log your name in the attendance sheet.

Step 2: Find a Book

Search the library catalog or browse the shelves to find the book you want to borrow.

Step 3: Borrow

Present the book and your ID at the circulation desk for check-out. Note the due date on the slip provided.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many books can I borrow at once?

Students may borrow up to 3 books at a time. Faculty and staff may have different borrowing privileges.

How long can I keep borrowed books?

The standard loan period is 7 days for students. Renewals may be possible if the book is not reserved by another user.

What if I lose or damage a book?

Please report lost or damaged books to the librarian immediately. Fines and replacement policies apply as stated in the library rules.

Can I return books after hours?

Yes, you may use the Book Drop located at the library entrance. Books returned after hours will be checked in the next business day.

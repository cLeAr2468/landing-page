Policies & Rules

Guidelines and regulations that ensure a conducive learning environment for all users.

Borrowing Policies

Guidelines for borrowing materials and managing your library account

Library Card Requirements

Valid student ID or staff identification required

One library card per person

Non-transferable to other individuals

Loan Periods

Books: 21 days
Reserve Materials: 2 hours
Reference Books: Library use only

Renewals & Returns

Up to 2 renewals if no holds

Return to any library location

Online renewal available

Conduct & Behavior

Standards of behavior to maintain a respectful and productive learning environment

Expected Behavior

Quiet Study Areas

Maintain silence in designated quiet zones

Respect for Others

Be considerate of fellow library users

Clean Environment

Keep study areas clean and organized

Prohibited Activities

Noise Disturbances

Loud conversations, phone calls, or music

Food & Drinks

Eating or drinking in library areas

Damaging Materials

Writing in or damaging library materials

Computer & Technology Use

Guidelines for using library computers, printers, and digital resources

Computer Access

Time Limit: 2 hours per session
Login Required: Student/Staff ID
Priority: Academic work first

Printing Services

Cost: $0.10 per page
Payment: Student account
Color Printing: $0.50 per page

Wi-Fi Access

Free Wi-Fi for students and staff

Secure network with login

No time restrictions

Fines & Penalties

Information about overdue fines and account restrictions

Overdue Fines

Regular Books

$0.25 per day, maximum $10.00 per item

Reserve Materials

$1.00 per hour, maximum $25.00 per item

Lost Materials

Replacement cost + $10.00 processing fee

Account Restrictions

Borrowing Suspension

Suspended when fines exceed $25.00

Graduation Holds

Outstanding fines must be paid before graduation

Payment Methods

Cash, check, or student account deduction

Study Room Policies

Guidelines for reserving and using library study rooms

Reservation Rules

Advance Booking: Up to 1 week
Time Limit: 3 hours max
Group Size: 2-6 people

Group Study

Quiet conversation allowed

Whiteboards available

Power outlets provided

Late Policy

15-minute grace period

Reservation forfeited if late

Available to walk-ins after grace period

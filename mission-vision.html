Brief History

Our journey from humble beginnings to becoming a modern learning resource center

1925

Foundation

The PIT Learning Resources Center was established as a small library serving the local community. Started with a modest collection of 500 books and a single reading room.

1950

Expansion Era

Major expansion project completed, adding specialized collections for engineering, technology, and applied sciences. Collection grew to over 10,000 volumes.

1980

Digital Transition

Implemented the first computerized catalog system (OPAC) and began digitizing rare collections. Introduced electronic databases and online resources.

2000

Modern Era

Complete digital transformation with online catalog, e-books, and virtual reference services. Launched mobile app and 24/7 digital access.

Present

Innovation Hub

Today, we serve as a comprehensive learning resource center with over 100,000 digital and physical resources, advanced research facilities, and innovative learning spaces designed for the 21st century.

PIT Library

Serving our community with knowledge and resources since 1925.

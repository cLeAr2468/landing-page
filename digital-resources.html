Printing & Photocopying

A photocopier is available in the library. The following is the schedule of fees:

Type Fee (Student/Non-Student)
Short bond paper P 1.50 (2.00)
Long bond paper P 2.00 (2.50)
Enlarged copy (short / long) P 4.00
Graphics / picture (short / long) P 3.00
  • Students have the option to photocopy the Filipiniana, reference or reserved books either in the library or outside of the library.
  • Books from the General Reference Section are strictly photocopied only in the library to avoid any losses from its set.
  • Photocopying outside the library should be done in not more than fifteen (15) minutes only.
